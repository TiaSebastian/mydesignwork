Living, working and studying on Gadigal Land.
Australian-Filipino maker. currently finishing a Bachelor of Design at UNSW: Majoring in Object and Textiles design.

My practice tends to shift depending on what I’m curious about at the time. I started out in dance and performing arts, but over time I found myself drawn to other ways of being creative. I’ve always liked making things - mostly out of curiosity, and that instinct still drives me. Right now I’m in a space of exploring new mediums and letting things evolve.
